Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 02, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Super Notes Pro ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The Super Notes Pro app is built to be an offline-first application, meaning your notes and personal data remain on your device.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on our servers.

All data created within the app (including notes, folders, and settings) is stored locally on your device using a local database (Hive).

2. App Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to provide specific features:

  • Notifications: Used solely to send you local reminders for your notes/tasks that you schedule.
  • Storage (Photos/Media/Files): Used solely for the "Backup & Restore" feature, allowing you to save a backup of your notes to your device or restore from a previously saved file. We do not access other files on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services that track users.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not store your data on our servers, you are in full control of your data. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, your locally stored notes will be deleted (unless you have created a backup file externally).
